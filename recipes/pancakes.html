Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for making pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggs
  • 150g plain flour
  • 120 ml semi skimmed milk
  • 30g caster sugar
  • 20g butter

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, add to the milk and beaten eggs. Whisk together.
  2. Add flour and sugar to a mixing bowl.
  3. Add the mixture to the bowl. Whisk until lumps are gone.
  4. Leave to set for 5 minutes while pan heats up.
  5. Ladle mixture into the pan. Cook until bubbles appear in mixture, then flip over for 1 minute.
  6. Remove pancakes.
  7. Eat pancakes.
  8. Lament that you have no more pancakes.
